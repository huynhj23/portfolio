Wash Me - A Car Washing App Prototype

Wash me is an application that is used by both the client and their customers for a local car washing business. Together with the client and other students in the class, we worked together to find all necessary functionalities such that the client can confirm an appointment, reschedule an appointment, locate customer's homes for home car washing services, and receive payment in the application, while the customer can book an appointment, get customer support, leave a review, and get an ETA for home car washing services.
